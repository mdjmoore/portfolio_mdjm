About

I'm Meagan Moore, a front-end web developer from Toronto. I've got a passion for making fun, accessible and streamlined sites.

As a former baker, I'm no stranger to fast-paced environments and keeping an organized prep list for the day. My creative outlet was always baking and developing new menus and when I was looking to change industries a few friends introduced me to web development and HackerYou. Building and debugging sites allows me to be just as creative, resilient and focused as before.

When I'm not coding, I can be found nose-deep in a new book, lifting heavy things and putting them back down, writing the next great novel in my notes app or playing roller derby with the Smoke City Bandits, Toronto's longest-running roller derby team.
